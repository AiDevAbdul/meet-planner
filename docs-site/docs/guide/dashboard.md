# Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first thing you see after signing in. It gives you a live snapshot of your work and your team.

**Available to:** All roles

---

## Stat Cards

Three summary cards appear at the top of the page:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Tasks Due Today** | Your tasks with today's due date |
| **Overdue Tasks** | Tasks past their due date assigned to you |
| **Unread Messages** | Total unread messages across all your channels |

Click any card to jump directly to the filtered view.

---

## My Tasks (Mini Kanban)

A condensed three-column board — **To Do / In Progress / Done** — showing only tasks assigned to you.

- Drag cards between columns to update status without opening the full Task Board.
- Click any card to open the full task detail.

---

## Recent Meetings

The three most recent meetings are listed with:
- Meeting title and date
- Source badge (Gmail / Manual / Google Meet)
- Number of extracted tasks

Click a meeting row to open its detail page.

---

## Team Activity Feed

A live stream of actions across the team:
- Tasks completed
- New meeting notes added
- Approvals granted
- Goal progress updated

Useful for staying informed without checking every section individually.

---

## Company Goals (OKR Progress)

Active company-level goals are displayed with a progress bar calculated from their Key Result completion. Click any goal to open its detail page and see the full breakdown.

:::tip
The Goals section shows only **company-level** goals here. Visit [Goals & OKRs](/docs/guide/goals) to manage department and individual goals.
:::
