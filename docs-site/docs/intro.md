# Welcome to MeetPlanner

MeetPlanner is an internal meeting and project planner. It converts meeting notes and transcripts into structured tasks, assigns them to team members, tracks deadlines, and keeps your team aligned through channels and goals.

## How it works

```
Meeting notes (paste / upload / Gmail)
        ↓
  AI extracts tasks, decisions, attendees
        ↓
  Manager reviews in Triage Queue
        ↓
  Tasks appear on the Team Board
        ↓
  Team works, tracks time, and closes tasks
```

## Signing in

1. Go to your MeetPlanner URL.
2. Click **Sign in with Google**.
3. Select your company Google Workspace account.
4. You land on your Dashboard.

:::info Domain restriction
Only email addresses on your company domain are accepted. Contact your Admin if access is denied.
:::

## Your role

Your role determines what you can see and do in MeetPlanner.

| Role | Summary |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage team, departments, automations, all content |
| **Manager** | Approve tasks and requests, triage AI extractions, view all analytics |
| **Member** | Create tasks, submit meeting notes, participate in messaging |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to tasks, meetings, and goals |

See the full breakdown in [Role Permissions](/docs/reference/permissions).

## Navigation

The left sidebar holds every section. The top bar has:

- **Global search** (`Cmd/Ctrl + K`) — find tasks, meetings, and people instantly
- **Notifications bell** — real-time alerts for mentions, approvals, and due dates
- **AI chat** (floating button, bottom-right) — conversational assistant with full team context
